German Sheperd

German Shepherds have been trained by military groups to parachute from aircraft or as anti-tank weapons.

  • Scientific Name: Canis Lupus Familiaris
  • Average Weight: Male - 67lbs. Female - 61lbs
  • Average Lifespan: 9 -13 years
  • Habitat: Domesticated working animal.

The German Shepherd Dog is a larger breed of dog. He stands between 22 and 26 inches tall at his shoulder. The GSD is longer than he is tall. The GSD's coat is a double coat of medium length. This coat typically has a black saddle with a body ranging from fawn to chocolate brown. While pure white or pure black colorings are possible in this breed, they are rare. The GSD has tall, pointed ears and a pointed, elongated snout.
